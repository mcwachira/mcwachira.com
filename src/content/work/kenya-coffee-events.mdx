Kenya Coffee Events is your one-stop platform for discovering and engaging with the exciting world of Kenyan coffee. We showcase a curated selection of coffee events happening across the country, from barista championships and tastings to farm tours and industry conferences.

Here's what you'll find on Kenya Coffee Events:

Comprehensive Event Listings: Stay informed about upcoming coffee events in Kenya, with detailed descriptions, dates, locations, and registration information.
Industry Promotion: We celebrate Kenyan coffee's rich heritage and vibrant future by highlighting roasters, cafes, competitions, and other coffee-related businesses.
Community Hub: Connect with fellow coffee enthusiasts, share your experiences, and learn more about Kenya's diverse coffee offerings.


Built with cutting-edge technology:
Next.js: Experience a fast, user-friendly, and dynamic website.
Sanity CMS: Explore a user-friendly content management system for effortless event updates.
